Why Perfume Strength Matters

Not all fragrances are created equal. Many commercial fragrances are Eau de Toilette, which contains a lower concentration of fragrance oils. Fusion Fragrances uses a higher fragrance oil concentration, which means the scent is richer, stronger, and lasts significantly longer on the skin.

Why Our Fragrances Last Longer

Longevity comes from the quality and concentration of fragrance oils used in the formula. Our perfumes contain a higher percentage of concentrated oils, allowing the scent to remain noticeable for many hours rather than fading quickly.

The Secret Behind the “Scent Cloud”

A great perfume does not just sit on the skin. It diffuses gently into the air, creating what perfumers call “sillage” – the scent trail that follows you. Fusion Fragrances are formulated to create a soft scent cloud that others can notice without being overpowering.

Luxury Inspired, Smartly Priced

Fusion Fragrances are inspired by some of the world’s most loved luxury scents. By focusing on the fragrance itself rather than expensive branding and packaging, we are able to offer high-quality, long-lasting perfumes at a far more accessible price.
